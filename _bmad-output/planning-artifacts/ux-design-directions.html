UX Design Directions (Streamlit Layouts)

Since we are strictly constrained to native Streamlit components, these directions explore different structural layout patterns for our Connection Wizard and Dashboard.

Direction 1: The Tabbed Single-Page

Uses st.tabs() to separate Setup, Governance, and Health in one view. High visibility, fast switching.
